What is ARDA?

This Autism Resource Database Alaska is a digital network for autism related resources specific to Alaska. The ARDA includes library items available for check out, reputable online items, services and therapies provided across Alaska, and upcoming events. The purpose for this database is to provide a hub for autism related information and services to help meet the needs of all Alaskans.

Know of a service provided and do not see it on our database?
Send us an e-mail at info@asagoldenheart.org and we will update our database!
