A Guide to Using ARDA

Step One

Select one or more categories by clicking "Resource Types" in the sidebar to the left and selecting the desired checkboxes from the resulting drop down menu.

Step Two

Further define your search by selecting one or more filters from the drop down menus: "Demographics", "Disorders", "Behaviors" and "Additional".

*If "Services" is selected an additional menu will automatically appear under "Select Filters" that only applies to service resources.

Step Three

Click the blue "Submit" button below the drop down menus. You will be showed resources on the results page pertaining to the filters you have selected. Click a resource to see its details.

Step Four

The resource details page will offer specific information about the resource, including how to access it. If it is a physical library item, such as a book or dvd, you can use the contact info at the bottom of the page to make an appointment to rent the item from our library!
